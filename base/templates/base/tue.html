Therapeutic Use Exemption(TUE)

In some situations, an athlete may have an illness or condition that requires the use of medication listed on the World Anti-Doping Agencyâ€™s Prohibited List. The TUE application process is thorough and designed to balance the need to provide athletes access to critical medication while protecting the rights of clean athletes to complete on a level playing field.

Four Criteria For Granting a TUE

  • Step 1: There is a significant medical requirement to use the prohibited substance or method.
  • Step 2: The prohibited substance or method cannot enhance performance past baseline levels.
  • Step 3: The prohibited substance or method is the only reasonable treatment available.
  • Step 4: The condition being treated was not caused by the previous misuse of a prohibited substance or method.

TUE Application Process

  • Step 1: Determine if a TUE is Needed.
  • Step 2: Find your Condition.
  • Step 3: Download the appropriate TUE application.
  • Step 4: Complete the TUE application and collect medical documents.
  • Step 5: Review your TUE application.
  • Step 6: Submit COMPLETED application to us
